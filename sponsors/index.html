Sponsorship Tiers

Friend Level ($150)

Our friend level tier is our basic sponsor tier! It gets your company name on all participant T-Shirts, 1 complimentary T-shirt, and recognition on our social media and website!

Blue ($250)

Our blue tier is our regular sponsor tier! It gets your company logo (medium-print) on all participant T-Shirts, 2 complimentary T-shirts, and recognition on our social media and website!

Gold ($350)

Our gold tier is our premium sponsor tier! It gets your company logo (large-print) on all participant T-Shirts, 2 complimentary T-Shirts, and recognition on our social media and website, as well as on race day!



All sponsors are encouraged to join us on race day and set up an information booth!
