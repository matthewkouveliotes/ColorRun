Sponsorship Tiers

Blue ($250)

Our blue sponsorship tier is our basic sponsor tier. It includes 2 free tickets and your company's logo will be small-print on all participants T-shirts!

Gold ($350)

Our gold sponsorship tier is our standard sponsor tier. It includes everything in the blue tier, however, your logo will be in medium print on all T-Shirts! You all also get multiple shoutouts throughout the race and a reserved vendor table during the event.

Colonial ($500)

Our Colonial sponsorship tier is our premium sponsor tier. It includes everything from the blue and gold sponsor tiers, but your logo will be in large print on all participant T-Shirts! Your logo will also be featured in social media posts, flyers, and in the photo booth backdrop!
